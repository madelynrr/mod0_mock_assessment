Class = Plant

Attributes:
roots (boolean)
leafColor (string)
leafType (string)
flowerType (string)
flowerNumber (integer)
currentHeight (integer or float)

Methods:
grow (currentHeight + 1)
changeLeafColor (changes the leaf color attribute)
prune (currentHeight - 1)
pickFlower (flowerNumber -1)


Class = Dog

Attributes:
furColor (string)
currentAge (integer)
currentWeight (float)
eyeColor (string)
loudBark (boolean)

Methods
wash (changes color attribute)
eat (currentWeight + 1.0)
age (currentAge + 1)
protect (loudBark attribute on)
